Controls


Keyboard:
  • Accelerate: Up arrow
  • Rotate: Left and right arrows
  • Fire the laser: Spacebar
  • Shield: Down arrow
Mouse:
  • Accelerate: Right click
  • Rotate: Left or right click
  • Fire the laser: Left click
  • Shield: Middle click
Controller:
  • Accelerate: Right face button
  • Rotate: Left analog stick
  • Fire the laser: Right trigger button
  • Shield: Left trigger button
